Why Tajweed Is Important

Tajweed protects the Quran from being changed. For example قلب means heart but if you mispronounce the Qaaf as Kaaf, you will say كلب which means dog. See how one small mistake completely changes the meaning?

4 Months Tajweed course

Basic Rules

Introduction to essential Tajweed rules like Makharij (points of articulation), Noon Saakin, and Tanween — for a correct foundation in recitation

Intermediate Tajweed

Covers rules such as Idghaam, Iqlaab, Ikhfaa, and Qalqalah with practical application in Surahs — refining pronunciation and fluency.

Advanced Tajweed

In-depth understanding of complex rules like Madd, Laam & Raa rulings, and characteristics of letters (Sifaat) to perfect recitation.

Practical Tilawah

Application of all learned rules through fluent recitation, correction by teacher, and building confidence to recite like the Qurra’.
